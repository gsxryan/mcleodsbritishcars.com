Cars for sale



All of our cars for sale have been gone over with a fine tooth comb and you will find a list of everything that is wrong with the car as well as what is new. It may look like a lot to some but, in our opinion, another car on the market will have a similar list but not all sellers will tell you. We understand many potential buyers do not live close enough to come and look at cars for themselves so we strive to describe them and photograph them in a way that there will be no surprises.

Check back regularly for updates, as we are changing inventory often. If you have any questions or would like to consign your car please feel free to contact us.

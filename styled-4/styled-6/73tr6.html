1973 Triumph TR6

-New radiator cap
-Tuned up (carbs balanced, etc)
-New correct screws in dash
-New windshield finisher
-New grille finisher
-Checked over all brakes and clutch systems
-Oil changed
-Good battery
-New thrust washers and rod bearings
-New tie rod ends and rack boots
-Fuel filter replaced
-New wiper washer bottle strap
-Suspension is good and tight
-Nice top
-New fuel tank sending unit

Issues with the car

-The floor had minor rust, the previous owner killed the rust and fiberglassed the floorboards over. We don't see any reason that this will cause any issues later but we still want you to know.
-Needs accelerator pedal bushings
-The crash pads and dash pad have some cracks in them
-The old brown panels were dyed and you can tell where it has been rubbed off in a few small spots.
-It does not have correct radiator hoses and could use some new ones.
-The rear end has not been boxed
-The rear wheels could use camber adjustment
-The paint under the hood is not the same shade of yellow as the rest of the car.
-The rear fender seam has been sealed between the rear cowl and fender.
-The paint is not very nice
-The rack does have some slack in it
-Older tires, not dry rotted just old


-The dash wiring harness is in need of attention which is leading to some electrical gremlins. We have a new harness in stock in case the buyer would like to buy it. We would recommend the harness being replaced.
